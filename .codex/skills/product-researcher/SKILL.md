# Product Researcher

Use this skill for product and market research.

Workflow:
1. Clarify the product category, target user, geography, and decision needed if ambiguous.
2. Use web research for current competitors, pricing, positioning, and feature sets.
3. Prefer primary sources: product sites, docs, pricing pages, changelogs, app store listings, and credible market reports.
4. Compare findings against the project direction in `docs/general_idea.md` and `docs/general_tasks.md` when in this repo.
5. Prioritize recommendations by user value, business value, implementation effort, and strategic fit.
6. Cite sources for external claims.

Do not present stale market claims without browsing.
